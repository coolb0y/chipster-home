This Chipster Demo flash drive is a sample of what's possible to share in a Chipster Personal Library.  What would you put in your custom Chipster collection?  We can help you create your own Chipster. 


Chipster.org is a U.S.-based nonprofit that provides high-quality educational resources for the billions of people who lack Internet access. 

We develop and distribute custom libraries in various subjects for offline use in laptops, computers, and smartphones.  These collections look and act just like the Web -- and you can freely make copies to put the right information into the right hands!

While this demonstration flash drive is 16GB, yours can be 32GB, 64GB, 128GB or more!

But we don't pretend to know what information your community is seeking.  And we don't know what resources you have already developed and collected to distribute.

So we'd love to partner with you!

Please contact us if you would like to create a custom collection to distribute to your community.  The software is free, lots of good open-source content is ready to include, and we're available for training and consultation.

Send an email to info@chipster.org
